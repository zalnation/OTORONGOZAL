Naemeh

"Art, for me, is a space where I explore the emotions of our human experience, translating ancient wisdom through thematic expression. I invite the viewer to immerse themselves in the convergence of the physical and spiritual realms, creating a state of bliss where the seeker becomes what they seek."

Originally from Iran I moved to California with my family at the age of 15. As a kid growing up art was an important form of creativity but for many years due to strict schooling in Iran Art took a backstreet for years and It was not until attending University that I started exploring the art of Design. With a focus on Graphic, Web, and Brand Design I graduated With a bachelor of Arts in Design from University of California, Davis. After Graduating I continued my work as a web Designer for a Secretary of State, California until I made the switch fully to freelance design and eventually creating another avenue of creative energy by teaching myself how to paint.

I now take my knowledge in Brand Strategy, Visual Identity and more and integrate that into My work flow as an abstract contemporary artist merging Art and Design where they both can shine
